## YOUR KIT — WHAT YOU NEED

You are equipped with the **Elegoo Super Starter Kit Uno R3**. Here is what is inside and when you will use it:

**Hardware (in your kit):**

- Arduino Uno R3 board — the brain of NEXUS (Act 2+)
- USB cable — connects the brain to your computer
- Breadboard — your build surface for circuits
- Jumper wires — the nerves that connect everything
- LEDs (red, yellow, green, and more) — used in Act 1 and beyond
- Resistors — protect your components from too much power
- Push buttons — used in Q05 and beyond
- Buzzer (active and passive) — used in Q06 and Q12
- RGB LED — used in SQ01
- Ultrasonic sensor (HC-SR04) — used in Q11
- Temperature and humidity sensor (DHT11) — used in Q13
- Potentiometer — used in Q10
- 9V battery and connector — used in Act 1 (no computer needed!)
- 7-segment display, LCD screen, servo motor — used in advanced quests

**Software (download before Act 2):**

- **Arduino IDE** — free download at [arduino.cc/en/software](https://arduino.cc/en/software)
  - This is the program you use to write code and send it to your Arduino
  - Ask a parent or helper to install it before Q07

## HOW TO PLAY

Each quest is its own mission file. Here is how every quest works:

1. **Read the Lore** — Every quest starts with a story. Read it to find out what part of NEXUS you are building and why it matters.

2. **Study the Law** — Each quest teaches one electronics law or concept. Read it carefully. This is the science behind what you are building.

3. **Review the Blueprint** — A diagram shows you how to wire your circuit before you touch anything. Study it first.

4. **Follow the Build Mission** — Step-by-step instructions walk you through building the circuit on your breadboard. Follow each step in order.

5. **Write the Code (Quest 8 and later)** — Starting in Act 2, you will write programs for your Arduino. Your quest file has the code and explains every line.

6. **Collect Your XP Reward** — When the quest works, you earn XP. Write it in your Mission Control sheet. Check the box. Add the robot part to NEXUS.

7. **Unlock What Is Next** — Check if any side quests have opened up. Update your character sheet. Move on to the next mission.

> **Tip:** If something does not work, that is normal. Real engineers troubleshoot. Check your wires, re-read the blueprint, and try again. NEXUS is counting on you.
